Card Augury

Card Augury is a data visualization project based on Magic: The Gathering, a trading card game. The game itself is now widely considered to be the world's most complex real-world game.

Card Augury is a project dedicated to breaking down raw data about the game (card types, sets, and secondary market price, etc.) to assist you in predicting future trends within the development of the game, and give you the latest in card statistics.
